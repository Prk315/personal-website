Scroll Effect Demo

As you scroll, the colorful sparks move in different directions based on their individual speed values. Each spark has its own trajectory, creating a dynamic parallax effect that responds to your scrolling.

The sparks also pulse, scale, and rotate as you scroll, creating a mesmerizing 3D-like effect that adds depth and interactivity to the background.

  • Layer 4: Gentle animated blobs (edges only)
  • Layer 5: Color-themed animated lines (8 total: warm/cool/purple)
